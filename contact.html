FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS.
EASY ANSWERS.

Is there a cover charge to enter?

We typically do not have a cover charge for entry. However, there may be special events or live performances where a cover charge applies.

Do you serve food?

Yes, we offer a selection of seafood and small plates to complement your drinks. Our menu includes appetizers, sandwiches, and other bar favorites.

What types of drinks do you serve?

We serve a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, including beer, wine, cocktails, spirits, and soft drinks. Our bar staff can also craft custom cocktails based on your preferences.

Do you have a beer or cocktail menu?

Yes, we have a beer and cocktail menu that features a selection of popular and signature drinks. Feel free to ask our bartenders for recommendations or to customize a drink to your liking.

Do you offer takeout or delivery?

No, this is a in person dining bar.

Do you have Wi-Fi available for customers?

Yes, we offer complimentary Wi-Fi for our customers. Just ask your server for the password.

Are gratuities included in the bill?

No, gratuities are not included in the bill. Tipping is at the discretion of the customer and greatly appreciated by our staff.
